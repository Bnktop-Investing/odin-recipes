Egglant Rolls With Ground Beef

Description

These aubergine rolls are stuffed with ground beef with mushrooms and bathed in avery rich tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Aubergine, medium cut into 8 slices
  • 7 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 kilos of ground beef, or assorted meat
  • 3 Slices whole wheat bread, toasted
  • 1/2 Sticks of butter
  • 125 Grams of mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 Springs of rosemary, the leaves separated from the twig and chopped
  • 3 Cups of beef broth
  • 3 Tablespoons of flour
  • 1 Tablespoons tomato paste

Prepatation

  1. Preheat a skillet with granting to grill the aubergines over medium-high heat.
  2. Varnish the slices of aubergines with ilive oil, sprinkle with and cook for 2 minutes on each side.
  3. heat a large skillet over high heat with 1/4 cup oil. Once it is hot, add the meat, with a wooden spoon, separate the meat so that it is in very small pieces. Cook until meat is cooked through and browned.
  4. While the meat is cooking, put the bread to toasted and cut into very small slices, reserve.
  5. Add the mushrooms to the pan whith the meat and cook with 1 teblespoon of the butter and minced garlic.
  6. Add the chopped rosemary and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Add the toast and 1 cup of the beef broth.
  8. Divide the mushroom and meat mixture between the eggplant slices. Roll each on starting from the smallest side. Repeat with the other 8 slices.
  9. In a medium skilled, melt the other 3 tablespoons of butter. Add the flour and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then add the tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  10. Add the beef broth to the skiller and cook for 5-6 minutes until thickened.
  11. Season with salt and pepper and serve on top of the eggplant rolls.
